Your Freindship for my Daughter has indeed been most powerfully exerted by throwing her into the arms of an unprincipled Fortunehunter.  (replied he)   Yes, (exclaimed I) amidst every misfortune, it will afford us some consolation to reflect that by this one act of Freindship to Janetta, we have amply discharged every obligation that we have received from her father. It must indeed be a most gratefull reflection, to your exalted minds.  (said he.)
