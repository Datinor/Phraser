If that is your opinion with regard to the girls it certainly is no proof of their Fathers beauty, for if they are perfectly unlike him and very handsome at the same time, it is natural to suppose that he is very plain. By no means, (said he) for what may be pretty in a Woman, may be very unpleasing in a Man. But you yourself (replied I) but a few minutes ago allowed him to be very plain. Men are no Judges of Beauty in their own Sex.  (said he).
