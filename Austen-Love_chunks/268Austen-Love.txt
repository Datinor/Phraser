And what did Mr Musgrove say to this? He replied that he was under a melancholy necessity of owning the truth of what I said, and that therefore if he should be the happy Creature destined to be the Husband of the Beautiful Henrietta he must bring himself to wait, however impatiently, for the fortunate day, when she might be freed from the power of worthless Relations and able to bestow herself on him.   What a noble Creature he is! Oh! Matilda what a fortunate one I am, who am to be his Wife!
