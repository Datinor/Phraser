 When we arrived at Warleigh, poor Augusta could scarcely breathe, while Margaret was all Life and Rapture.  The longexpected Moment is now arrived (said she) and we shall soon be in the World. --In a few Moments we were in Mrs Cope's parlour, where with her daughter she sate ready to receive us. I observed with delight the impression my Children made on them--. They were indeed two sweet, elegantlooking Girls, and tho' somewhat abashed from the peculiarity of their situation, yet there was an ease in their Manners and address which could not fail of pleasing--.
