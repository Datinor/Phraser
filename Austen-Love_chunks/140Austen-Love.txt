LETTER the SIXTH LADY LESLEY to Miss CHARLOTTE LUTTERELL LesleyCastle March 20th  We arrived here my sweet Freind about a fortnight ago, and I already heartily repent that I ever left our charming House in Portmansquare for such a dismal old weatherbeaten Castle as this. You can form no idea sufficiently hideous, of its dungeonlike form. It is actually perched upon a Rock to appearance so totally inaccessible, that I expected to have been pulled up by a rope.
