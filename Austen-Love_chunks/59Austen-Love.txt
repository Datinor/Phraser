LETTER the 12th LAURA in continuation  You may imagine how greatly we were surprised by the sudden departure of Lord St Clair.  So far from having ever declared it, I have no reason to imagine that he has ever felt any for me. That he certainly adores you (replied Sophia) there can be no doubt--. The Attachment must be reciprocal. Did he never gaze on you with admiration--tenderly press your hand--drop an involantary tear--and leave the room abruptly?
