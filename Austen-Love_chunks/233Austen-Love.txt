Perfect Felicity is not the property of Mortals, and no one has a right to expect uninterrupted Happiness.--Some Misfortunes I have certainly met with. WHAT Misfortunes dear Ma'am? replied I, burning with impatience to know every thing.  NONE Ma'am I hope that have been the effect of any wilfull fault in me. I dare say not Ma'am, and have no doubt but that any sufferings you may have experienced could arise only from the cruelties of Relations or the Errors of Freinds.
