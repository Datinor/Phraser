I had then a Father and a Mother, and had never known misfortunes--But now deprived of every freind but you-- What! (interrupted Augusta) is my Brother dead then? Tell us I intreat you what is become of him? Yes, cold and insensible Nymph, (replied I) that luckless swain your Brother, is no more, and you may now glory in being the Heiress of Sir Edward's fortune.
