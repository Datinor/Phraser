She plays, sings and Dances, but has no taste for either, and excells in none, tho' she says she is passionately fond of all. Perhaps you may flatter me so far as to be surprised that one of whom I speak with so little affection should be my particular freind. but to tell you the truth, our freindship arose rather from Caprice on her side than Esteem on mine.
