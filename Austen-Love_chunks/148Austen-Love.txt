Neither Men nor Women can think Sir George tolerable. Well, well, (said he) we will not dispute about HIS Beauty, but your opinion of his DAUGHTERS is surely very singular, for if I understood you right, you said you did not find them so plain as you expected to do! Why, do YOU find them plainer then?  (said I).   I can scarcely beleive you to be serious (returned he) when you speak of their persons in so extroidinary a Manner.
