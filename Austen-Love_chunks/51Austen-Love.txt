To your Mother I should have returned with Pleasure, should have been happy to have introduced to her, my charming Sophia and should with Chearfullness have passed the remainder of my Life in their dear Society in the Vale of Uske, had not one obstacle to the execution of so agreable a scheme, intervened. which was the Marriage and Removal of your Mother to a distant part of Ireland. Adeiu Laura. LETTER 11th LAURA in continuation   I have a Relation in Scotland (said Sophia to me as we left London) who I am certain would not hesitate in receiving me.
