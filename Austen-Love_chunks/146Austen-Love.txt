Oh! pray dont mind me. (replied I) every one knows Sir George is horribly ugly, and I assure you I always thought him a fright. You surprise me extremely (answered William) by what you say both with respect to Sir George and his Daughters. You cannot think your Husband so deficient in personal Charms as you speak of, nor can you surely see any resemblance between him and the Miss Lesleys who are in my opinion perfectly unlike him and perfectly Handsome.
