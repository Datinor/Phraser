--continued her--   Oh Lady Scudamore, said I wiping my eyes, that such a sweet Creature should talk of dieing! It is an affecting Circumstance indeed, replied Lady Scudamore. When I am dead said he, let me be carried and lain at her feet, and perhaps she may not disdain to drop a pitying tear on my poor remains. Dear Lady Scudamore interrupted I, say no more on this affecting subject.
