I who am doomed to waste my Days of Youth and Beauty in an humble Cottage in the Vale of Uske.   Ah! little did I then think I was ordained so soon to quit that humble Cottage for the Deceitfull Pleasures of the World. Adeiu Laura. LETTER 5th LAURA to MARIANNE  One Evening in December as my Father, my Mother and myself, were arranged in social converse round our Fireside, we were on a sudden greatly astonished, by hearing a violent knocking on the outward door of our rustic Cot.
