I am now going to murder my Sister. Yours Ever, Anna Parker. A TOUR THROUGH WALES--in a LETTER from a YOUNG LADY--  My Dear Clara I have been so long on the ramble that I have not till now had it in my power to thank you for your Letter--. We left our dear home on last Monday month. and proceeded on our tour through Wales, which is a principality contiguous to England and gives the title to the Prince of Wales.
