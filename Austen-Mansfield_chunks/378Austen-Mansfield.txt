Norris gave the particulars of the letters, and the subject was dropt. but after tea, as Miss Crawford was standing at an open window with Edmund and Fanny looking out on a twilight scene, while the Miss Bertrams, Rushworth, and Henry Crawford were all busy with candles at the pianoforte, she suddenly revived it by turning round towards the group, and saying,  How happy Rushworth looks! He is thinking of November.
