  Fanny read to herself that  it was with infinite concern the newspaper had to announce to the world a matrimonial fracas in the family of R. of Wimpole Street. the beautiful R., whose name had not long been enrolled in the lists of Hymen, and who had promised to become so brilliant a leader in the fashionable world, having quitted her husband's roof in company with the wellknown and captivating C., the intimate friend and associate of R., and it was not known even to the editor of the newspaper whither they were gone. It is a mistake, sir, it must be a mistake, it cannot be true. it must mean some other people.
