I see nothing alarming in the word. Did you think I ought? Perhaps, sir, perhaps, sir, I thought it was a pity you did not always know yourself as well as you seemed to do at that moment.   Crawford, delighted to get her to speak at any rate, was determined to keep it up. and poor Fanny, who had hoped to silence him by such an extremity of reproof, found herself sadly mistaken, and that it was only a change from one object of curiosity and one set of words to another.
