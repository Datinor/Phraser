She was beyond their reach. and if at last obliged to yield--no matter--it was all misery now. CHAPTER XVII  It was, indeed, a triumphant day to Bertram and Maria. Such a victory over Edmund's discretion had been beyond their hopes, and was most delightful. There was no longer anything to disturb them in their darling project, and they congratulated each other in private on the jealous weakness to which they attributed the change, with all the glee of feelings gratified in every way.
