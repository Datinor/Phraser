If I had known you were going out, I should have got you just to go as far as my house with some orders for Nanny, which I have since, to my very great inconvenience, been obliged to go and carry myself. I could very ill spare the time, and you might have saved me the trouble, if you would only have been so good as to let us know you were going out.
