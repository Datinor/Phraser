  Crawford took the volume.  Let me have the pleasure of finishing that speech to your ladyship, I shall find it immediately.  And by carefully giving way to the inclination of the leaves, he did find it, or within a page or two, quite near enough to satisfy Lady Bertram, who assured him, as soon as he mentioned the name of Cardinal Wolsey, that he had got the very speech. Not a look or an offer of help had Fanny given. not a syllable for or against.
