She could not but own that she should be very glad of a little tea, and Susan immediately set about making it, as if pleased to have the employment all to herself. and with only a little unnecessary bustle, and some few injudicious attempts at keeping her brothers in better order than she could, acquitted herself very well. Fanny's spirit was as much refreshed as her body. her head and heart were soon the better for such welltimed kindness. Susan had an open, sensible countenance.
