Rushworth is never remiss. But dear Maria has such a strict sense of propriety, so much of that true delicacy which one seldom meets with nowadays, Rushworth--that wish of avoiding particularity! Dear ma'am, only look at her face at this moment. how different from what it was the two last dances!   Miss Bertram did indeed look happy, her eyes were sparkling with pleasure, and she was speaking with great animation, for Julia and her partner, Crawford, were close to her.
