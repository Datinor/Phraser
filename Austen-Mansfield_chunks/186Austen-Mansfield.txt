I would have everything as complete as possible in the country, shrubberies and flowergardens, and rustic seats innumerable: but it must all be done without my care. Henry is different. he loves to be doing.   Edmund was sorry to hear Miss Crawford, whom he was much disposed to admire, speak so freely of her uncle. It did not suit his sense of propriety, and he was silenced, till induced by further smiles and liveliness to put the matter by for the present.
