And could nobody be employed on such an errand but Fanny? Upon my word, ma'am, it has been a very illmanaged business. I am sure I do not know how it was to have been done better, unless I had gone myself, indeed. but I cannot be in two places at once. and I was talking to Mr. Green at that very time about your mother's dairymaid, by her desire, and had promised John Groom to write to Jefferies about his son, and the poor fellow was waiting for me half an hour.
