But they had seen no one from the Parsonage, not a creature, and had heard no tidings beyond a friendly note of congratulation and inquiry from Grant to Lady Bertram. It was the first day for many, many weeks, in which the families had been wholly divided. Fourandtwenty hours had never passed before, since August began, without bringing them together in some way or other. It was a sad, anxious day.
