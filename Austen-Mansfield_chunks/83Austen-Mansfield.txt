And I am quite convinced that your being with Norris will be as good for your mind as riding has been for your health, and as much for your ultimate happiness too.   So ended their discourse, which, for any very appropriate service it could render Fanny, might as well have been spared, for Norris had not the smallest intention of taking her. It had never occurred to her, on the present occasion, but as a thing to be carefully avoided. To prevent its being expected, she had fixed on the smallest habitation which could rank as genteel among the buildings of Mansfield parish, the White House being only just large enough to receive herself and her servants, and allow a spare room for a friend, of which she made a very particular point.
