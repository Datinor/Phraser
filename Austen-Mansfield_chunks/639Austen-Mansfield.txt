The removal of the bookcase from before the billiardroom door struck him especially, but he had scarcely more than time to feel astonished at all this, before there were sounds from the billiardroom to astonish him still farther. Some one was talking there in a very loud accent. he did not know the voice--more than talking--almost hallooing. He stepped to the door, rejoicing at that moment in having the means of immediate communication, and, opening it, found himself on the stage of a theatre, and opposed to a ranting young man, who appeared likely to knock him down backwards.
