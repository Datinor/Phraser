And round their enormous great wide table, too, which fills up the room so dreadfully! Had the doctor been contented to take my diningtable when I came away, as anybody in their senses would have done, instead of having that absurd new one of his own, which is wider, literally wider than the dinnertable here, how infinitely better it would have been! and how much more he would have been respected! for people are never respected when they step out of their proper sphere. Remember that, Fanny.
