On the contrary, I told him, I cannot recollect my exact words, but I am sure I told him that I would not listen to him, that it was very unpleasant to me in every respect, and that I begged him never to talk to me in that manner again. I am sure I said as much as that and more. and I should have said still more, if I had been quite certain of his meaning anything seriously. but I did not like to be, I could not bear to be, imputing more than might be intended. I thought it might all pass for nothing with him.
