She told him that she did not love him, could not love him, was sure she never should love him. that such a change was quite impossible. that the subject was most painful to her. that she must entreat him never to mention it again, to allow her to leave him at once, and let it be considered as concluded for ever. And when farther pressed, had added, that in her opinion their dispositions were so totally dissimilar as to make mutual affection incompatible.
