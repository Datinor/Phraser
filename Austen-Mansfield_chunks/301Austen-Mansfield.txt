  While this was passing, the rest of the party being scattered about the chapel, Julia called Crawford's attention to her sister, by saying,  Do look at Rushworth and Maria, standing side by side, exactly as if the ceremony were going to be performed. Have not they completely the air of it?   Crawford smiled his acquiescence, and stepping forward to Maria, said, in a voice which she only could hear,  I do not like to see Miss Bertram so near the altar.
