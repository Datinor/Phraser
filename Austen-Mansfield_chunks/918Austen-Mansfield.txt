The chain will agree with William's cross beyond all comparison better than the necklace. For one night, Fanny, for only one night, if it be a sacrifice. I am sure you will, upon consideration, make that sacrifice rather than give pain to one who has been so studious of your comfort. Miss Crawford's attentions to you have been--not more than you were justly entitled to--I am the last person to think that could be, but they have been invariable. and to be returning them with what must have something the air of ingratitude, though I know it could never have the meaning, is not in your nature, I am sure.
