I know her disposition to be as sweet and faultless as your own, but the influence of her former companions makes her seem--gives to her conversation, to her professed opinions, sometimes a tinge of wrong. She does not think evil, but she speaks it, speaks it in playfulness. and though I know it to be playfulness, it grieves me to the soul. The effect of education, Yes, that uncle and aunt! They have injured the finest mind.
