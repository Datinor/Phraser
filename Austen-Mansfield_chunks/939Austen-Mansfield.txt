I have almost given up every serious idea of her. but I must be a blockhead indeed, if, whatever befell me, I could think of your kindness and sympathy without the sincerest gratitude.   He had said enough to shake the experience of eighteen. He had said enough to give Fanny some happier feelings than she had lately known, and with a brighter look, she answered,  Yes, cousin, I am convinced that you would be incapable of anything else, though perhaps some might not.
