Now, Edmund, do not be disagreeable, Nobody loves a play better than you do, or can have gone much farther to see one. True, to see real acting, good hardened real acting. but I would hardly walk from this room to the next to look at the raw efforts of those who have not been bred to the trade: a set of gentlemen and ladies, who have all the disadvantages of education and decorum to struggle through.
