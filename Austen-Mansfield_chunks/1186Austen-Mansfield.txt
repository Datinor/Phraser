And will tell you what, Fanny, which is more than I did for Maria: the next time Pug has a litter you shall have a puppy.     CHAPTER XXXIV  Edmund had great things to hear on his return. Many surprises were awaiting him. The first that occurred was not least in interest: the appearance of Henry Crawford and his sister walking together through the village as he rode into it. He had concluded--he had meant them to be far distant.
