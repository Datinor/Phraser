The situation of the house excluded the possibility of much prospect from any of the rooms. and while Fanny and some of the others were attending Rushworth, Henry Crawford was looking grave and shaking his head at the windows. Every room on the west front looked across a lawn to the beginning of the avenue immediately beyond tall iron palisades and gates. Having visited many more rooms than could be supposed to be of any other use than to contribute to the windowtax, and find employment for housemaids,  Now, we are coming to the chapel, which properly we ought to enter from above, and look down upon.
