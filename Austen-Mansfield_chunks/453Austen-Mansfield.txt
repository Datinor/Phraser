No want of understrappers: my sister desires her love, and hopes to be admitted into the company, and will be happy to take the part of any old duenna or tame confidante, that you may not like to do yourselves.   Maria gave Edmund a glance, which meant,  What say you now? Can we be wrong if Mary Crawford feels the same?  And Edmund, silenced, was obliged to acknowledge that the charm of acting might well carry fascination to the mind of genius.
