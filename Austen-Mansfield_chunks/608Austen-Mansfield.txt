Tom was enjoying such an advance towards the end. Edmund was in spirits from the morning's rehearsal, and little vexations seemed everywhere smoothed away. All were alert and impatient. the ladies moved soon, the gentlemen soon followed them, and with the exception of Lady Bertram, Norris, and Julia, everybody was in the theatre at an early hour. and having lighted it up as well as its unfinished state admitted, were waiting only the arrival of Grant and the Crawfords to begin.
