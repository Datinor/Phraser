  This, though the thought of the moment, did not end with the moment. for the inclination to act was awakened, and in no one more strongly than in him who was now master of the house. and who, having so much leisure as to make almost any novelty a certain good, had likewise such a degree of lively talents and comic taste, as were exactly adapted to the novelty of acting. The thought returned again and again.  Oh for the Ecclesford theatre and scenery to try something with.
