William's promotion, with all its particulars, he was soon master of. and with such a secret provision of comfort within his own breast to help the joy, he found in it a source of most gratifying sensation and unvarying cheerfulness all dinnertime. After dinner, when he and his father were alone, he had Fanny's history. and then all the great events of the last fortnight, and the present situation of matters at Mansfield were known to him. Fanny suspected what was going on.
