Upon the whole, Julia was in the best looks of the two, at least after you were spoken of. There was no recovering the complexion from the moment that I spoke of 'Fanny,' and spoke of her as a sister should. But Rushworth's day of good looks will come. we have cards for her first party on the 28th. Then she will be in beauty, for she will open one of the best houses in Wimpole Street.
