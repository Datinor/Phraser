Norris is much better fitted than my mother for having the charge of you now. She is of a temper to do a great deal for anybody she really interests herself about, and she will force you to do justice to your natural powers.   Fanny sighed, and said,  I cannot see things as you do. but I ought to believe you to be right rather than myself, and I am very much obliged to you for trying to reconcile me to what must be.
