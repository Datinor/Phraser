Why, you know, Sir Thomas's means will be rather straitened if the Antigua estate is to make such poor returns. Oh! that will soon be settled. Sir Thomas has been writing about it, I know. Well, Lady Bertram, I can only say that my sole desire is to be of use to your family: and so, if Sir Thomas should ever speak again about my taking Fanny, you will be able to say that my health and spirits put it quite out of the question.
