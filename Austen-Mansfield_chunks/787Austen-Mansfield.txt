and Fanny, averting her face, said, with a firmer tone than usual,  As far as I am concerned, sir, I would not have delayed his return for a day. My uncle disapproved it all so entirely when he did arrive, that in my opinion everything had gone quite far enough.   She had never spoken so much at once to him in her life before, and never so angrily to any one. and when her speech was over, she trembled and blushed at her own daring.
