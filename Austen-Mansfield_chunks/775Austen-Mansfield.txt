  There was no occasion, there was no time for Fanny to say how very differently she felt. but the idea of having such another to observe her was a great increase of the trepidation with which she performed the very awful ceremony of walking into the drawingroom. In the drawingroom Crawford certainly was, having been just long enough arrived to be ready for dinner. and the smiles and pleased looks of the three others standing round him, shewed how welcome was his sudden resolution of coming to them for a few days on leaving Bath. A very cordial meeting passed between him and Edmund.
