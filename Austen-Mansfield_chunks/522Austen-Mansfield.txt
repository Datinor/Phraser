I do not like my situation: this place is too hot for me, Never mind, my dear Miss Price, this is a cross evening: everybody is cross and teasing, but do not let us mind them . and with pointed attention continued to talk to her and endeavour to raise her spirits, in spite of being out of spirits herself. By a look at her brother she prevented any farther entreaty from the theatrical board, and the really good feelings by which she was almost purely governed were rapidly restoring her to all the little she had lost in Edmund's favour. Fanny did not love Miss Crawford.
