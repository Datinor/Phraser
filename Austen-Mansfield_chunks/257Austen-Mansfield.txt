Between ourselves, Edmund, it was cutting the roses, and dawdling about in the flowergarden, that did the mischief. I am afraid it was, indeed, I am very much afraid she caught the headache there, for the heat was enough to kill anybody. It was as much as I could bear myself. Sitting and calling to Pug, and trying to keep him from the flowerbeds, was almost too much for me.   Edmund said no more to either lady.
