For a moment he ceased, but she had barely become conscious of it, when, rising from his chair, he said,  And now, Fanny, having performed one part of my commission, and shewn you everything placed on a basis the most assured and satisfactory, I may execute the remainder by prevailing on you to accompany me downstairs, where, though I cannot but presume on having been no unacceptable companion myself, I must submit to your finding one still better worth listening to. Crawford, as you have perhaps foreseen, is yet in the house. He is in my room, and hoping to see you there.
