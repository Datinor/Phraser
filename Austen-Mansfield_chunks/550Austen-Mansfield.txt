But I could not be easy till I had spoken to you, and come to a decision. Sleeping or waking, my head has been full of this matter all night. It is an evil, but I am certainly making it less than it might be. If Tom is up, I shall go to him directly and get it over, and when we meet at breakfast we shall be all in high goodhumour at the prospect of acting the fool together with such unanimity. You, in the meanwhile, will be taking a trip into China, I suppose.
