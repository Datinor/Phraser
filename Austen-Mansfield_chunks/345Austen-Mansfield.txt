But unluckily that iron gate, that haha, give me a feeling of restraint and hardship. 'I cannot get out,' as the starling said.  As she spoke, and it was with expression, she walked to the gate: he followed her.  Mr. Rushworth is so long fetching this key! And for the world you would not get out without the key and without Mr. Rushworth's authority and protection, or I think you might with little difficulty pass round the edge of the gate, here, with my assistance.
