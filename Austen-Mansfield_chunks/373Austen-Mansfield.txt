I shall get the dairymaid to set them under the first spare hen, and if they come to good I can have them moved to my own house and borrow a coop. and it will be a great delight to me in my lonely hours to attend to them. And if I have good luck, your mother shall have some.   It was a beautiful evening, mild and still, and the drive was as pleasant as the serenity of Nature could make it.
