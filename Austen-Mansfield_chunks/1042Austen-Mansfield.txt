I wish the discovery may do them any good. And they will now see their cousin treated as she ought to be, and I wish they may be heartily ashamed of their own abominable neglect and unkindness. They will be angry, Mrs. Rushworth will be very angry. It will be a bitter pill to her. that is, like other bitter pills, it will have two moments' ill flavour, and then be swallowed and forgotten.
