It was but to think of her conversation with Edmund, and what was the restlessness of Norris? What were the yawns of Lady Bertram? The gentlemen joined them. and soon after began the sweet expectation of a carriage, when a general spirit of ease and enjoyment seemed diffused, and they all stood about and talked and laughed, and every moment had its pleasure and its hope. Fanny felt that there must be a struggle in Edmund's cheerfulness, but it was delightful to see the effort so successfully made.
