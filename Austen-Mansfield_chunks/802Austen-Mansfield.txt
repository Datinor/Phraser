You do not seem properly aware of her claims to notice. When we talked of her last night, you none of you seemed sensible of the wonderful improvement that has taken place in her looks within the last six weeks. You see her every day, and therefore do not notice it. but I assure you she is quite a different creature from what she was in the autumn. She was then merely a quiet, modest, not plainlooking girl, but she is now absolutely pretty.
