An afterpiece instead of a comedy, Lovers' Vows were at an end, and Lord and Lady Ravenshaw left to act My Grandmother by themselves. Well, the jointure may comfort him. and perhaps, between friends, he began to tremble for his credit and his lungs in the Baron, and was not sorry to withdraw. and to make you amends, Yates, I think we must raise a little theatre at Mansfield, and ask you to be our manager.
