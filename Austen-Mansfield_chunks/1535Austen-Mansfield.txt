It was a sort of playing at being frightened. The sufferings which Lady Bertram did not see had little power over her fancy. and she wrote very comfortably about agitation, and anxiety, and poor invalids, till Tom was actually conveyed to Mansfield, and her own eyes had beheld his altered appearance. Then a letter which she had been previously preparing for Fanny was finished in a different style, in the language of real feeling and alarm. then she wrote as she might have spoken.
