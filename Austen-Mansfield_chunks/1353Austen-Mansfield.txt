She had tears for every room in the house, much more for every beloved inhabitant. She clung to her aunt, because she would miss her. she kissed the hand of her uncle with struggling sobs, because she had displeased him. and as for Edmund, she could neither speak, nor look, nor think, when the last moment came with him. and it was not till it was over that she knew he was giving her the affectionate farewell of a brother.
