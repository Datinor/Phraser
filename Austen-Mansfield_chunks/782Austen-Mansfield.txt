And then changing his tone again to one of gentle gallantry, and addressing Fanny, he said,  You were Rushworth's best friend. Your kindness and patience can never be forgotten, your indefatigable patience in trying to make it possible for him to learn his part--in trying to give him a brain which nature had denied--to mix up an understanding for him out of the superfluity of your own!
