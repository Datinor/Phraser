When the subject was brought forward again, her views were more fully explained. and, in reply to Lady Bertram's calm inquiry of  Where shall the child come to first, sister, to you or to us?  Sir Thomas heard with some surprise that it would be totally out of Norris's power to take any share in the personal charge of her. He had been considering her as a particularly welcome addition at the Parsonage, as a desirable companion to an aunt who had no children of her own.
