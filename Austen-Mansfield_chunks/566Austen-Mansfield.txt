Fanny's heart was not absolutely the only saddened one amongst them, as she soon began to acknowledge to herself. Julia was a sufferer too, though not quite so blamelessly. Henry Crawford had trifled with her feelings. but she had very long allowed and even sought his attentions, with a jealousy of her sister so reasonable as ought to have been their cure.
