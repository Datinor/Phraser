I think I shall have Repton. Rushworth, if I were you, I would have a very pretty shrubbery. One likes to get out into a shrubbery in fine weather.   Rushworth was eager to assure her ladyship of his acquiescence, and tried to make out something complimentary. but, between his submission to her taste, and his having always intended the same himself, with the superadded objects of professing attention to the comfort of ladies in general, and of insinuating that there was one only whom he was anxious to please, he grew puzzled, and Edmund was glad to put an end to his speech by a proposal of wine.
