He took care, however, that they should be allowed to go to the shops they came out expressly to visit. and it did not delay them long, for Fanny could so little bear to excite impatience, or be waited for, that before the gentlemen, as they stood at the door, could do more than begin upon the last naval regulations, or settle the number of threedeckers now in commission, their companions were ready to proceed. They were then to set forward for the dockyard at once, and the walk would have been conducted--according to Crawford's opinion--in a singular manner, had Price been allowed the entire regulation of it, as the two girls, he found, would have been left to follow, and keep up with them or not, as they could, while they walked on together at their own hasty pace. He was able to introduce some improvement occasionally, though by no means to the extent he wished.
