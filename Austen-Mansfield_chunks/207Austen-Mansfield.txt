Here are many that would be interested in your improvements, my dear Rushworth, and that would like to hear Crawford's opinion on the spot, and that might be of some small use to you with their opinions. and, for my own part, I have been long wishing to wait upon your good mother again. nothing but having no horses of my own could have made me so remiss.
