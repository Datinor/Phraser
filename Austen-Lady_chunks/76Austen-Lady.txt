After I had sent off my letter to you, I went to Vernon, and sat with him in his room talking over the whole matter, and then determined to look for Frederica, whom I had not seen since breakfast. I met her on the stairs, and saw that she was crying.  My dear aunt, he is going--De Courcy is going, and it is all my fault.
