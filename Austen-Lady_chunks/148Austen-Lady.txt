After such a discovery as this, you will scarcely affect further wonder at my meaning in bidding you adieu. My understanding is at length restored, and teaches no less to abhor the artifices which had subdued me than to despise myself for the weakness on which their strength was founded. R. DE COURCY. XXXVII   LADY SUSAN TO MR. DE COURCY   Upper Seymour Street.
