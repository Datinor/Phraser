XVI   LADY SUSAN TO MRS. JOHNSON   Churchhill. Never, my dearest Alicia, was I so provoked in my life as by a letter this morning from Miss Summers. That horrid girl of mine has been trying to run away. I had not a notion of her being such a little devil before, she seemed to have all the Vernon milkiness. but on receiving the letter in which I declared my intention about Sir James, she actually attempted to elope.
