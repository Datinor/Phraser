I should have feared, too, for her health, and for everything but her principles--there I believe she is not to be injured by her mother, or her mother's friends. but with those friends she must have mixed (a very bad set, I doubt not), or have been left in total solitude, and I can hardly tell which would have been worse for her. If she is with her mother, moreover, she must, alas! in all probability be with Reginald, and that would be the greatest evil of all.
