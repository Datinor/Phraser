Flexibility of mind, a disposition easily biassed by others, is an attribute which you know I am not very desirous of obtaining. nor has Frederica any claim to the indulgence of her notions at the expense of her mother's inclinations. Her idle love for Reginald, too! It is surely my duty to discourage such romantic nonsense. All things considered, therefore, it seems incumbent on me to take her to town and marry her immediately to Sir James.
