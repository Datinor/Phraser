It is not for us to blame any expectations on your father's side of your marrying to advantage. where possessions are so extensive as those of your family, the wish of increasing them, if not strictly reasonable, is too common to excite surprize or resentment. He has a right to require. a woman of fortune in his daughterinlaw, and I am sometimes quarrelling with myself for suffering you to form a connection so imprudent. but the influence of reason is often acknowledged too late by those who feel like me.
