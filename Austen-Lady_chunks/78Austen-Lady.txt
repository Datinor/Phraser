But what is it you have done to occasion all this?  She blushed deeply as she answered:  I was so unhappy about Sir James that I could not help--I have done something very wrong, I know. but you have not an idea of the misery I have been in: and mamma had ordered me never to speak to you or my uncle about it, and-- You therefore spoke to my brother to engage his interference, No, but I wrote to him--I did indeed, I got up this morning before it was light, and was two hours about it.
