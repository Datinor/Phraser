Well, you give him credit for more simple, disinterested benevolence in this instance than I do. for while Miss Bates was speaking, a suspicion darted into my head, and I have never been able to get it out again. The more I think of it, the more probable it appears. In short, I have made a match between Knightley and Jane Fairfax. See the consequence of keeping you company!--What do you say to it?
