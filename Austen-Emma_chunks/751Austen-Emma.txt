Upon the whole, she was very persuadable. and it being briefly settled among themselves how it might be done without neglecting his comfort--how certainly Goddard, if not Bates, might be depended on for bearing him company--Woodhouse was to be talked into an acquiescence of his daughter's going out to dinner on a day now near at hand, and spending the whole evening away from him.
