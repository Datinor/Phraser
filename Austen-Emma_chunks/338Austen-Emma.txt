I have no hesitation in saying, I have no hesitation in saying--at least if my friend feels at all as I do--I have not the smallest doubt that, could he see his little effusion honoured as I see it, (looking at the book again, and replacing it on the table), he would consider it as the proudest moment of his life.   After this speech he was gone as soon as possible. Emma could not think it too soon.
