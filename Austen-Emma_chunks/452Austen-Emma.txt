I think you will agree with me, (turning with a soft air to Emma,) I think I shall certainly have your approbation, though Knightley perhaps, from being used to the large parties of London, may not quite enter into our feelings. I know nothing of the large parties of London, sir--I never dine with any body. Indeed! (in a tone of wonder and pity,) I had no idea that the law had been so great a slavery.
