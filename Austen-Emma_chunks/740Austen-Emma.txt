There was certainly no harm in his travelling sixteen miles twice over on such an errand. but there was an air of foppery and nonsense in it which she could not approve. It did not accord with the rationality of plan, the moderation in expense, or even the unselfish warmth of heart, which she had believed herself to discern in him yesterday. Vanity, extravagance, love of change, restlessness of temper, which must be doing something, good or bad. heedlessness as to the pleasure of his father and Weston, indifferent as to how his conduct might appear in general.
