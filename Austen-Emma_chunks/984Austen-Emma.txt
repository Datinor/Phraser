When once it had been read, there was no doing any thing, but lament and exclaim. The loss of the ball--the loss of the young man--and all that the young man might be feeling!--It was too wretched!--Such a delightful evening as it would have been!--Every body so happy! and she and her partner the happiest!-- I said it would be so, Of all horrid things, leavetaking is the worst. But you will come again, This will not be your only visit to Randalls.
