It was certainly never brilliant, but she would not allow it to have a sickly hue in general. and there was a softness and delicacy in her skin which gave peculiar elegance to the character of her face.  He listened with all due deference. acknowledged that he had heard many people say the same--but yet he must confess, that to him nothing could make amends for the want of the fine glow of health. Where features were indifferent, a fine complexion gave beauty to them all.
