She was, of course, the object of their joint dislike.--When they had nothing else to say, it must be always easy to begin abusing Miss Woodhouse. and the enmity which they dared not shew in open disrespect to her, found a broader vent in contemptuous treatment of Harriet. Elton took a great fancy to Jane Fairfax. and from the first. Not merely when a state of warfare with one young lady might be supposed to recommend the other, but from the very first.
