Every thing that I have said or done, for many weeks past, has been with the sole view of marking my adoration of yourself. You cannot really, seriously, doubt it. No!--(in an accent meant to be insinuating)--I am sure you have seen and understood me.   It would be impossible to say what Emma felt, on hearing this--which of all her unpleasant sensations was uppermost.
