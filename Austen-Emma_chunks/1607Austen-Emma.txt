Every moment had brought a fresh surprize. and every surprize must be matter of humiliation to her.--How to understand it all! How to understand the deceptions she had been thus practising on herself, and living under!--The blunders, the blindness of her own head and heart!--she sat still, she walked about, she tried her own room, she tried the shrubbery--in every place, every posture, she perceived that she had acted most weakly. that she had been imposed on by others in a most mortifying degree. that she had been imposing on herself in a degree yet more mortifying.
