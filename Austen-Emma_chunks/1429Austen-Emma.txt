She laughed because she was disappointed. and though she liked him for his attentions, and thought them all, whether in friendship, admiration, or playfulness, extremely judicious, they were not winning back her heart. She still intended him for her friend.   How much I am obliged to you, for telling me to come today!--If it had not been for you, I should certainly have lost all the happiness of this party. I had quite determined to go away again.
