Frank turned instantly to Emma, to claim her former promise. and boasted himself an engaged man, which his father looked his most perfect approbation of--and it then appeared that Weston was wanting him to dance with Elton himself, and that their business was to help to persuade him into it, which was done pretty soon.--Weston and Mrs. Elton led the way, Frank Churchill and Miss Woodhouse followed.
