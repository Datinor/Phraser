Emma could have wished Mrs. Elton elsewhere. but she was in a humour to have patience with every body. and as Elton met her with unusual graciousness, she hoped the rencontre would do them no harm. She soon believed herself to penetrate Elton's thoughts, and understand why she was, like herself, in happy spirits. it was being in Miss Fairfax's confidence, and fancying herself acquainted with what was still a secret to other people.
