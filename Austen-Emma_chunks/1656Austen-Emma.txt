I have very little to say for my own conduct.--I was tempted by his attentions, and allowed myself to appear pleased.--An old story, probably--a common case--and no more than has happened to hundreds of my sex before. and yet it may not be the more excusable in one who sets up as I do for Understanding. Many circumstances assisted the temptation.
