You ought to know your friend best, but I should say she was a goodtempered, softhearted girl, not likely to be very, very determined against any young man who told her he loved her.   Emma could not help laughing as she answered,  Upon my word, I believe you know her quite as well as I do.--But, Knightley, are you perfectly sure that she has absolutely and downright accepted him. I could suppose she might in time--but can she already?--Did not you misunderstand him?--You were both talking of other things.
