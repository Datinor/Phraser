CHAPTER X   Though now the middle of December, there had yet been no weather to prevent the young ladies from tolerably regular exercise. and on the morrow, Emma had a charitable visit to pay to a poor sick family, who lived a little way out of Highbury. Their road to this detached cottage was down Vicarage Lane, a lane leading at right angles from the broad, though irregular, main street of the place.
