I merely called, because I would not pass the door without inquiring after Bates. but I have been so pleasantly detained! Now, however, we must wish you and Bates good morning.   And not all that could be urged to detain her succeeded. She regained the street--happy in this, that though much had been forced on her against her will, though she had in fact heard the whole substance of Jane Fairfax's letter, she had been able to escape the letter itself.
