I see no sign of attachment--I believe nothing of the pianoforte--and proof only shall convince me that Knightley has any thought of marrying Jane Fairfax.   They combated the point some time longer in the same way. Emma rather gaining ground over the mind of her friend. for Weston was the most used of the two to yield. till a little bustle in the room shewed them that tea was over, and the instrument in preparation.--and at the same moment Cole approaching to entreat Miss Woodhouse would do them the honour of trying it.
