The tone implied some old acquaintance--and how could she possibly guess?   Knightley! Knightley himself!--Was not it lucky?--for, not being within when he called the other day, I had never seen him before. and of course, as so particular a friend of E.'s, I had a great curiosity. 'My friend Knightley' had been so often mentioned, that I was really impatient to see him.
