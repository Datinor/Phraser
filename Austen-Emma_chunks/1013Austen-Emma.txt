It was well to have a comfort in store on Harriet's behalf, though it might be wise to let the fancy touch it seldom. for evil in that quarter was at hand. As Frank Churchill's arrival had succeeded Elton's engagement in the conversation of Highbury, as the latest interest had entirely borne down the first, so now upon Frank Churchill's disappearance, Elton's concerns were assuming the most irresistible form.--His weddingday was named. He would soon be among them again.
