Knightley?--Who was so useful to him, who so ready to write his letters, who so glad to assist him?--Who so cheerful, so attentive, so attached to him?--Would not he like to have him always on the spot?--Yes. That was all very true. Knightley could not be there too often. he should be glad to see him every day.--but they did see him every day as it was.--Why could not they go on as they had done? Woodhouse could not be soon reconciled.
