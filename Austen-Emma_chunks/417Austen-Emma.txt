And she talked in this way so long and successfully that, when forced to give her attention again to her father and sister, she had nothing worse to hear than Isabella's kind inquiry after Jane Fairfax. and Jane Fairfax, though no great favourite with her in general, she was at that moment very happy to assist in praising.   That sweet, amiable Jane Fairfax!
