I made her take her shawl--for the evenings are not warm--her large new shawl-- Dixon's weddingpresent.--So kind of her to think of my mother! Bought at Weymouth, you know--Dixon's choice. There were three others, Jane says, which they hesitated about some time. Colonel Campbell rather preferred an olive. My dear Jane, are you sure you did not wet your feet?--It was but a drop or two, but I am so afraid:--but Frank Churchill was so extremely--and there was a mat to step upon--I shall never forget his extreme politeness.--Oh!
