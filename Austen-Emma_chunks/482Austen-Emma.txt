  Emma saw Weston's surprize, and felt that it must be great, at an address which, in words and manner, was assuming to himself the right of first interest in her. and as for herself, she was too much provoked and offended to have the power of directly saying any thing to the purpose. She could only give him a look. but it was such a look as she thought must restore him to his senses, and then left the sofa, removing to a seat by her sister, and giving her all her attention. She had not time to know how Elton took the reproof, so rapidly did another subject succeed.
