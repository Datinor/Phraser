My good friend, this is quite unnecessary. Frank knows a puddle of water when he sees it, and as to Bates's, he may get there from the Crown in a hop, step, and jump.   They were permitted to go alone. and with a cordial nod from one, and a graceful bow from the other, the two gentlemen took leave. Emma remained very well pleased with this beginning of the acquaintance, and could now engage to think of them all at Randalls any hour of the day, with full confidence in their comfort.
