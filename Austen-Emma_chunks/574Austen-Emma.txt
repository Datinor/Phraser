My mother's are really very good indeed. Jane often says, when she is here, 'I am sure, grandmama, you must have had very strong eyes to see as you do--and so much fine work as you have done too!--I only wish my eyes may last me as well.'   All this spoken extremely fast obliged Miss Bates to stop for breath. and Emma said something very civil about the excellence of Miss Fairfax's handwriting.   you who are such a judge, and write so beautifully yourself.
