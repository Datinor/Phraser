Pray, Emma, may I ask in what lay the great amusement, the poignant sting of the last word given to you and Miss Fairfax? I saw the word, and am curious to know how it could be so very entertaining to the one, and so very distressing to the other.   Emma was extremely confused. She could not endure to give him the true explanation. for though her suspicions were by no means removed, she was really ashamed of having ever imparted them.
