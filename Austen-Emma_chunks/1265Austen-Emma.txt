When she was halfway up the set, the whole group were exactly behind her, and she would no longer allow her eyes to watch. but Mr. Elton was so near, that she heard every syllable of a dialogue which just then took place between him and Weston. and she perceived that his wife, who was standing immediately above her, was not only listening also, but even encouraging him by significant glances.--The kindhearted, gentle Weston had left her seat to join him and say,  Do not you dance, Elton?
