He has a wife and family to maintain, and is not to be giving away his time. Well, now I have just given you a hint of what Jane writes about, we will turn to her letter, and I am sure she tells her own story a great deal better than I can tell it for her. I am afraid we must be running away, My father will be expecting us. I had no intention, I thought I had no power of staying more than five minutes, when I first entered the house.
