and of their having eight cows, two of them Alderneys, and one a little Welch cow, a very pretty little Welch cow indeed. and of Martin's saying as she was so fond of it, it should be called her cow. and of their having a very handsome summerhouse in their garden, where some day next year they were all to drink tea:--a very handsome summerhouse, large enough to hold a dozen people.   For some time she was amused, without thinking beyond the immediate cause. but as she came to understand the family better, other feelings arose.
