Not one of them had the power of removal, or of effecting any material change of society. They must encounter each other, and make the best of it. Harriet was farther unfortunate in the tone of her companions at Mrs. Goddard's. Elton being the adoration of all the teachers and great girls in the school. and it must be at Hartfield only that she could have any chance of hearing him spoken of with cooling moderation or repellent truth.
