I mean good Perry!--such attention to Jane! --And from her great, her more than commonly thankful delight towards Elton for being there, Emma guessed that there had been a little show of resentment towards Jane, from the vicarage quarter, which was now graciously overcome.--After a few whispers, indeed, which placed it beyond a guess, Elton, speaking louder, said,   Yes, here I am, my good friend. and here I have been so long, that anywhere else I should think it necessary to apologise.
