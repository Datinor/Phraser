For the world would not she have seemed to threaten me.--Imagine the shock. imagine how, till I had actually detected my own blunder, I raved at the blunders of the post.--What was to be done?--One thing only.--I must speak to my uncle. Without his sanction I could not hope to be listened to again.--I spoke. circumstances were in my favour.
