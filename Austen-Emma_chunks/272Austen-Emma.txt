It was by no means his daughter's wish that the intellects of Highbury in general should be put under requisition. Elton was the only one whose assistance she asked. He was invited to contribute any really good enigmas, charades, or conundrums that he might recollect. and she had the pleasure of seeing him most intently at work with his recollections.
