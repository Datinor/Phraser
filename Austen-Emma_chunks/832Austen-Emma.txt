A real injury to the children--a most mortifying change, and material loss to them all.--a very great deduction from her father's daily comfort--and, as to herself, she could not at all endure the idea of Jane Fairfax at Donwell Abbey. A Mrs. Knightley for them all to give way to!--No--Knightley must never marry. Little Henry must remain the heir of Donwell. Presently Knightley looked back, and came and sat down by her. They talked at first only of the performance.
