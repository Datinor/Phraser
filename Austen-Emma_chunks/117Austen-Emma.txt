And he was really a very pleasing young man, a young man whom any woman not fastidious might like. He was reckoned very handsome. his person much admired in general, though not by her, there being a want of elegance of feature which she could not dispense with:--but the girl who could be gratified by a Robert Martin's riding about the country to get walnuts for her might very well be conquered by Elton's admiration.
