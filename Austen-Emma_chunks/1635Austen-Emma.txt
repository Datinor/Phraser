I am always well, you know. Be sure to give me intelligence of the letter as soon as possible.   Weston's communications furnished Emma with more food for unpleasant reflection, by increasing her esteem and compassion, and her sense of past injustice towards Miss Fairfax. She bitterly regretted not having sought a closer acquaintance with her, and blushed for the envious feelings which had certainly been, in some measure, the cause. Had she followed Knightley's known wishes, in paying that attention to Miss Fairfax, which was every way her due.
