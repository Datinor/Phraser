Ah, my dear, poor Miss Taylor--It is a grievous business. Oh yes, sir, how you must miss her! And dear Emma, too!--What a dreadful loss to you both!--I have been so grieved for you.--I could not imagine how you could possibly do without her.--It is a sad change indeed.--But I hope she is pretty well, sir. Pretty well, my dear--I hope--pretty well.--I do not know but that the place agrees with her tolerably.   John Knightley here asked Emma quietly whether there were any doubts of the air of Randalls.
