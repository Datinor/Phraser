What a thinking brain you have! I say, Jane, what a perfect character you and I should make, if we could be shaken together. My liveliness and your solidity would produce perfection.--Not that I presume to insinuate, however, that some people may not think you perfection already.--But hush!--not a word, if you please.   It seemed an unnecessary caution. Jane was wanting to give her words, not to Elton, but to Miss Woodhouse, as the latter plainly saw.
