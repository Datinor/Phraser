There it is. There go you and your riddlebook one of these days. --Harriet's was--   Oh, what a sweet house!--How very beautiful!--There are the yellow curtains that Miss Nash admires so much. I do not often walk this way now, but then there will be an inducement, and I shall gradually get intimately acquainted with all the hedges, gates, pools and pollards of this part of Highbury.
