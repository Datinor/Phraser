You, Emma, who have so few opportunities of dancing, you are really out of luck. you are very much out of luck!   It was some days before she saw Jane Fairfax, to judge of her honest regret in this woeful change. but when they did meet, her composure was odious. She had been particularly unwell, however, suffering from headache to a degree, which made her aunt declare, that had the ball taken place, she did not think Jane could have attended it.
