Elton was to take the drawing to London, chuse the frame, and give the directions. and Emma thought she could so pack it as to ensure its safety without much incommoding him, while he seemed mostly fearful of not being incommoded enough.   This man is almost too gallant to be in love, I should say so, but that I suppose there may be a hundred different ways of being in love. He is an excellent young man, and will suit Harriet exactly.
