She was pleased, on taking leave, to find Miss Fairfax determined to attend her out of the room, to go with her even downstairs. it gave her an opportunity which she immediately made use of, to say,   It is as well, perhaps, that I have not had the possibility. Had you not been surrounded by other friends, I might have been tempted to introduce a subject, to ask questions, to speak more openly than might have been strictly correct.--I feel that I should certainly have been impertinent.
