And I will own to you, (I am sure it will be safe), that so far as our living with Churchill at Enscombe, it is settled. There must be three months, at least, of deep mourning. but when they are over, I imagine there will be nothing more to wait for. Thank you, thank you.--This is just what I wanted to be assured of.--Oh! if you knew how much I love every thing that is decided and open!--Goodbye, goodbye.
