To be sure, so it is. But they live very comfortably. They have no indoors man, else they do not want for any thing. and Martin talks of taking a boy another year. I wish you may not get into a scrape, Harriet, whenever he does marry.--I mean, as to being acquainted with his wife--for though his sisters, from a superior education, are not to be altogether objected to, it does not follow that he might marry any body at all fit for you to notice.
