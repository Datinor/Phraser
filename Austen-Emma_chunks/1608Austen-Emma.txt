To that point went every leisure moment which her father's claims on her allowed, and every moment of involuntary absence of mind. How long had Knightley been so dear to her, as every feeling declared him now to be? When had his influence, such influence begun?-- When had he succeeded to that place in her affection, which Frank Churchill had once, for a short period, occupied?--She looked back.
