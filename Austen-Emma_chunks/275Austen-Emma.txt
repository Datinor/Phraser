He called for a few moments, just to leave a piece of paper on the table containing, as he said, a charade, which a friend of his had addressed to a young lady, the object of his admiration, but which, from his manner, Emma was immediately convinced must be his own.   I do not offer it for Miss Smith's collection, Being my friend's, I have no right to expose it in any degree to the public eye, but perhaps you may not dislike looking at it.
