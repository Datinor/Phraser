A disagreeable truth would be palatable through her lips, but I am the wretchedest being in the world at a civil falsehood. I do not believe any such thing, I am persuaded that you can be as insincere as your neighbours, when it is necessary. but there is no reason to suppose the instrument is indifferent. Quite otherwise indeed, if I understood Miss Fairfax's opinion last night. Do come with me, if it be not very disagreeable to you.
