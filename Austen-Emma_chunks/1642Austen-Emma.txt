All that were good would be withdrawn. and if to these losses, the loss of Donwell were to be added, what would remain of cheerful or of rational society within their reach? Knightley to be no longer coming there for his evening comfort!--No longer walking in at all hours, as if ever willing to change his own home for their's!--How was it to be endured? And if he were to be lost to them for Harriet's sake. if he were to be thought of hereafter, as finding in Harriet's society all that he wanted.
