We consider our Hartfield pork, indeed it certainly is, so very superior to all other pork, that Emma and I cannot have a greater pleasure than-- Oh! my dear sir, as my mother says, our friends are only too good to us. If ever there were people who, without having great wealth themselves, had every thing they could wish for, I am sure it is us. We may well say that 'our lot is cast in a goodly heritage.'
