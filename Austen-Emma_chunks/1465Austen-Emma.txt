Such another scheme, composed of so many illassorted people, she hoped never to be betrayed into again. While waiting for the carriage, she found Knightley by her side. He looked around, as if to see that no one were near, and then said,   Emma, I must once more speak to you as I have been used to do: a privilege rather endured than allowed, perhaps, but I must still use it. I cannot see you acting wrong, without a remonstrance.
