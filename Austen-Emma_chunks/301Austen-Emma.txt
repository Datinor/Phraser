And he, the very handsomest man that ever was, and a man that every body looks up to, quite like Knightley! His company so sought after, that every body says he need not eat a single meal by himself if he does not chuse it. that he has more invitations than there are days in the week. And so excellent in the Church! Miss Nash has put down all the texts he has ever preached from since he came to Highbury.
