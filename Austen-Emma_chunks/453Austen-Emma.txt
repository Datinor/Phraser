Well, sir, the time must come when you will be paid for all this, when you will have little labour and great enjoyment. My first enjoyment, will be to find myself safe at Hartfield again.     CHAPTER XIV   Some change of countenance was necessary for each gentleman as they walked into Weston's drawingroom.--Elton must compose his joyous looks, and John Knightley disperse his illhumour. Mr. Elton must smile less, and John Knightley more, to fit them for the place.--Emma only might be as nature prompted, and shew herself just as happy as she was. To her it was real enjoyment to be with the Westons.
