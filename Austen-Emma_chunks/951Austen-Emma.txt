I can answer for every thing of that nature, sir, because it will be under Weston's care. Weston undertakes to direct the whole. There, papa!--Now you must be satisfied--Our own dear Weston, who is carefulness itself. Do not you remember what Perry said, so many years ago, when I had the measles? 'If Miss Taylor undertakes to wrap Miss Emma up, you need not have any fears, sir.'
