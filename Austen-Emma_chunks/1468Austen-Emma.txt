I wish you could have heard her honouring your forbearance, in being able to pay her such attentions, as she was for ever receiving from yourself and your father, when her society must be so irksome. Oh! I know there is not a better creature in the world: but you must allow, that what is good and what is ridiculous are most unfortunately blended in her. They are blended, I acknowledge. and, were she prosperous, I could allow much for the occasional prevalence of the ridiculous over the good.
