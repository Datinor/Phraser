Only one more, papa. only for Elton. Poor Elton! You like Mr. Elton, papa,--I must look about for a wife for him. There is nobody in Highbury who deserves him--and he has been here a whole year, and has fitted up his house so comfortably, that it would be a shame to have him single any longer--and I thought when he was joining their hands today, he looked so very much as if he would like to have the same kind office done for him!
