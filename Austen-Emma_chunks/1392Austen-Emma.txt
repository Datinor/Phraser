In this walk Emma and Weston found all the others assembled. and towards this view she immediately perceived Knightley and Harriet distinct from the rest, quietly leading the way. Knightley and Harriet!--It was an odd tetea-tete. but she was glad to see it.--There had been a time when he would have scorned her as a companion, and turned from her with little ceremony. Now they seemed in pleasant conversation.
