Emma saw symptoms of it immediately in the expression of her face. and while paying her own compliments to Mrs. Bates, and appearing to attend to the good old lady's replies, she saw her with a sort of anxious parade of mystery fold up a letter which she had apparently been reading aloud to Miss Fairfax, and return it into the purple and gold reticule by her side, saying, with significant nods,   We can finish this some other time, you know. You and I shall not want opportunities. And, in fact, you have heard all the essential already.
