Our part of London is very superior to most others!--You must not confound us with London in general, my dear sir. The neighbourhood of Brunswick Square is very different from almost all the rest. We are so very airy! I should be unwilling, I own, to live in any other part of the town.--there is hardly any other that I could be satisfied to have my children in: but we are so remarkably airy!--Wingfield thinks the vicinity of Brunswick Square decidedly the most favourable as to air.
