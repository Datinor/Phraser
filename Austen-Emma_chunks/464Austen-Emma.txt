Emma liked the subject so well, that she began upon it, to Weston, very soon after their moving into the drawingroom: wishing her joy--yet observing, that she knew the first meeting must be rather alarming.-- Weston agreed to it. but added, that she should be very glad to be secure of undergoing the anxiety of a first meeting at the time talked of:  for I cannot depend upon his coming.
