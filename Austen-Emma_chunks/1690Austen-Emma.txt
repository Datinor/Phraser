She must tell you herself what she is--yet not by word, for never was there a human creature who would so designedly suppress her own merit.--Since I began this letter, which will be longer than I foresaw, I have heard from her.--She gives a good account of her own health. but as she never complains, I dare not depend. I want to have your opinion of her looks. I know you will soon call on her. she is living in dread of the visit.
