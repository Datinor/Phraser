Till I have a letter from Colonel Campbell, I can imagine nothing with any confidence. It must be all conjecture. Conjecture--aye, sometimes one conjectures right, and sometimes one conjectures wrong. I wish I could conjecture how soon I shall make this rivet quite firm. What nonsense one talks, Miss Woodhouse, when hard at work, if one talks at all.--your real workmen, I suppose, hold their tongues.
