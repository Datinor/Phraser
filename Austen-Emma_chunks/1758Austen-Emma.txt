The wish of distinguishing her, as far as civility permitted, was very evident, though it could not often proceed beyond a look. Elton made his appearance. His lady greeted him with some of her sparkling vivacity.   Very pretty, sir, upon my word. to send me on here, to be an encumbrance to my friends, so long before you vouchsafe to come!--But you knew what a dutiful creature you had to deal with.
