Now, upon his father's marriage, it was very generally proposed, as a most proper attention, that the visit should take place. There was not a dissentient voice on the subject, either when Perry drank tea with and Miss Bates, or when and Miss Bates returned the visit. Now was the time for Frank Churchill to come among them. and the hope strengthened when it was understood that he had written to his new mother on the occasion. For a few days, every morning visit in Highbury included some mention of the handsome letter Weston had received.
