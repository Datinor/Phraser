I have the pleasure, madam, (to Bates,) of restoring your spectacles, healed for the present.   He was very warmly thanked both by mother and daughter. to escape a little from the latter, he went to the pianoforte, and begged Miss Fairfax, who was still sitting at it, to play something more.
