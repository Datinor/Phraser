I dare say he will come in when he knows who is here. Quite delightful to have you all meet so!--Our little room so honoured!   She was in the adjoining chamber while she still spoke, and opening the casement there, immediately called Knightley's attention, and every syllable of their conversation was as distinctly heard by the others, as if it had passed within the same apartment.   How d' ye do?--how d'ye do?--Very well, I thank you. So obliged to you for the carriage last night.
