  At this moment Weston appeared at a little distance from the window, evidently on the watch. His wife gave him a look which invited him in. and, while he was coming round, added,  Now, dearest Emma, let me intreat you to say and look every thing that may set his heart at ease, and incline him to be satisfied with the match. Let us make the best of it--and, indeed, almost every thing may be fairly said in her favour.
