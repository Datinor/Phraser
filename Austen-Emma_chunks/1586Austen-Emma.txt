Harriet, who was standing at some distance, and with face turned from her, did not immediately say any thing. and when she did speak, it was in a voice nearly as agitated as Emma's.   I should not have thought it possible, that you could have misunderstood me! I know we agreed never to name him--but considering how infinitely superior he is to every body else, I should not have thought it possible that I could be supposed to mean any other person. Frank Churchill, indeed!
