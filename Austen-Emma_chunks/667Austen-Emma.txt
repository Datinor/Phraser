By that time, it was beginning to hold up, and I was determined that nothing should stop me from getting away--and then--only think!--I found he was coming up towards me too--slowly you know, and as if he did not quite know what to do. and so he came and spoke, and I answered--and I stood for a minute, feeling dreadfully, you know, one can't tell how.
