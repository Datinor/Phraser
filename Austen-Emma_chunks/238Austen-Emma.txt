But I could not reason so to a man in love, and was willing to trust to there being no harm in her, to her having that sort of disposition, which, in good hands, like his, might be easily led aright and turn out very well. The advantage of the match I felt to be all on her side. and had not the smallest doubt (nor have I now) that there would be a general cryout upon her extreme good luck. Even your satisfaction I made sure of.
