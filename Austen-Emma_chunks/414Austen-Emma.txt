but I assure you, excepting those little nervous headaches and palpitations which I am never entirely free from anywhere, I am quite well myself. and if the children were rather pale before they went to bed, it was only because they were a little more tired than usual, from their journey and the happiness of coming. I hope you will think better of their looks tomorrow. for I assure you Wingfield told me, that he did not believe he had ever sent us off altogether, in such good case. I trust, at least, that you do not think Knightley looking ill, Middling, my dear.
