He never wished to attach me. It was merely a blind to conceal his real situation with another.--It was his object to blind all about him. and no one, I am sure, could be more effectually blinded than myself--except that I was not blinded--that it was my good fortune--that, in short, I was somehow or other safe from him.   She had hoped for an answer here--for a few words to say that her conduct was at least intelligible. but he was silent.
