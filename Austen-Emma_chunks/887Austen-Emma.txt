Miss Bates had just done as Patty opened the door. and her visitors walked upstairs without having any regular narration to attend to, pursued only by the sounds of her desultory goodwill.   Pray take care, Weston, there is a step at the turning. Pray take care, Miss Woodhouse, ours is rather a dark staircase--rather darker and narrower than one could wish.
