It is the greatest amusement in the world! And after such success, you know!--Every body said that Weston would never marry again. Oh dear, no! Weston, who had been a widower so long, and who seemed so perfectly comfortable without a wife, so constantly occupied either in his business in town or among his friends here, always acceptable wherever he went, always cheerful--Weston need not spend a single evening in the year alone if he did not like it. Oh no!
