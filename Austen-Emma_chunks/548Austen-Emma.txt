Such language for a young man entirely dependent, to use!--Nobody but you, Knightley, would imagine it possible. But you have not an idea of what is requisite in situations directly opposite to your own. Frank Churchill to be making such a speech as that to the uncle and aunt, who have brought him up, and are to provide for him!--Standing up in the middle of the room, I suppose, and speaking as loud as he could!--How can you imagine such conduct practicable?
