The same civilities and courtesies, the same success and satisfaction, took place on the morrow, and accompanied the whole progress of the picture, which was rapid and happy. Every body who saw it was pleased, but Elton was in continual raptures, and defended it through every criticism.   Miss Woodhouse has given her friend the only beauty she wanted, --observed Weston to him--not in the least suspecting that she was addressing a lover.--
