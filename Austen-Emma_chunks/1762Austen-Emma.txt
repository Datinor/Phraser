And no apology left, no message for me. The housekeeper declared she knew nothing of my being expected.--Very extraordinary!--And nobody knew at all which way he was gone. Perhaps to Hartfield, perhaps to the Abbey Mill, perhaps into his woods.--Miss Woodhouse, this is not like our friend Knightley!--Can you explain it?   Emma amused herself by protesting that it was very extraordinary, indeed, and that she had not a syllable to say for him.   I cannot imagine, I cannot imagine how he could do such a thing by you, of all people in the world!
