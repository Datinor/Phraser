It is a sort of thing which nobody could have expected. I am sure, a month ago, I had no more idea myself!--The strangest things do take place! When Miss Smiths and Eltons get acquainted--they do indeed--and really it is strange. it is out of the common course that what is so evidently, so palpably desirable--what courts the prearrangement of other people, should so immediately shape itself into the proper form. You and Elton are by situation called together.
