There had been a time also when Emma would have been sorry to see Harriet in a spot so favourable for the Abbey Mill Farm. but now she feared it not. It might be safely viewed with all its appendages of prosperity and beauty, its rich pastures, spreading flocks, orchard in blossom, and light column of smoke ascending.--She joined them at the wall, and found them more engaged in talking than in looking around.
