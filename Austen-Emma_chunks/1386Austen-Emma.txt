In the meanwhile the lame horse recovered so fast, that the party to Box Hill was again under happy consideration. and at last Donwell was settled for one day, and Box Hill for the next,--the weather appearing exactly right. Under a bright midday sun, at almost Midsummer, Woodhouse was safely conveyed in his carriage, with one window down, to partake of this alfresco party.
