I do not know, my dear--but it is so long since she was here!--not since last Easter, and then only for a few days.--John Knightley's being a lawyer is very inconvenient.--Poor Isabella!--she is sadly taken away from us all!--and how sorry she will be when she comes, not to see Miss Taylor here! She will not be surprized, papa, at least. I do not know, my dear.
