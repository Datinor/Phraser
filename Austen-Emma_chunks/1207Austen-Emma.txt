After tea, and Weston, and Elton sat down with Mr. Woodhouse to cards. The remaining five were left to their own powers, and Emma doubted their getting on very well. for Knightley seemed little disposed for conversation. Elton was wanting notice, which nobody had inclination to pay, and she was herself in a worry of spirits which would have made her prefer being silent. John Knightley proved more talkative than his brother.
