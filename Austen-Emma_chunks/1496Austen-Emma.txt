It is not every body that would have stood out in such a kind way as she did, and refuse to take Jane's answer. but she positively declared she would not write any such denial yesterday, as Jane wished her. she would wait--and, sure enough, yesterday evening it was all settled that Jane should go. Quite a surprize to me! I had not the least idea!--Jane took Elton aside, and told her at once, that upon thinking over the advantages of Smallridge's situation, she had come to the resolution of accepting it.--I did not know a word of it till it was all settled.
