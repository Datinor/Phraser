How you can bear such recollections, is astonishing to me!--They will sometimes obtrude--but how you can court them!   He had a great deal to say in return, and very entertainingly. but Emma's feelings were chiefly with Jane, in the argument. and on leaving Randalls, and falling naturally into a comparison of the two men, she felt, that pleased as she had been to see Frank Churchill, and really regarding him as she did with friendship, she had never been more sensible of Knightley's high superiority of character. The happiness of this most happy day, received its completion, in the animated contemplation of his worth which this comparison produced.
