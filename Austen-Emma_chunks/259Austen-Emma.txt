I have heard him speak with great animation of a large family of young ladies that his sisters are intimate with, who have all twenty thousand pounds apiece. I am very much obliged to you, If I had set my heart on Elton's marrying Harriet, it would have been very kind to open my eyes. but at present I only want to keep Harriet to myself. I have done with matchmaking indeed.
