I knew it was--but had not resolution enough to part with them. But, Harriet, is it necessary to burn the courtplaister?--I have not a word to say for the bit of old pencil, but the courtplaister might be useful. I shall be happier to burn it, It has a disagreeable look to me. I must get rid of every thing.--There it goes, and there is an end, thank Heaven! of Elton.
