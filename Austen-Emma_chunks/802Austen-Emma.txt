No, that is no subject of regret at all. I have no pleasure in seeing my friends, unless I can believe myself fit to be seen.   The rest of the gentlemen being now in the room, Emma found herself obliged to turn from him for a few minutes, and listen to Cole. When Cole had moved away, and her attention could be restored as before, she saw Frank Churchill looking intently across the room at Miss Fairfax, who was sitting exactly opposite.   Thank you for rousing me, I believe I have been very rude.
