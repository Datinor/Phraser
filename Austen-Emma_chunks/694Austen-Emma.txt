 His son, too well bred to hear the hint, rose immediately also, saying,   As you are going farther on business, sir, I will take the opportunity of paying a visit, which must be paid some day or other, and therefore may as well be paid now. I have the honour of being acquainted with a neighbour of yours, (turning to Emma,) a lady residing in or near Highbury. a family of the name of Fairfax. I shall have no difficulty, I suppose, in finding the house.
