Emma encouraged her talkativeness--amused by such a picture of another set of beings, and enjoying the youthful simplicity which could speak with so much exultation of Martin's having  two parlours, two very good parlours, indeed. one of them quite as large as Goddard's drawingroom. and of her having an upper maid who had lived fiveandtwenty years with her.
