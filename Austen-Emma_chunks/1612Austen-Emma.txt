Such a debasement on his! It was horrible to Emma to think how it must sink him in the general opinion, to foresee the smiles, the sneers, the merriment it would prompt at his expense. the mortification and disdain of his brother, the thousand inconveniences to himself.--Could it be?--No. it was impossible. And yet it was far, very far, from impossible.--Was it a new circumstance for a man of firstrate abilities to be captivated by very inferior powers?
