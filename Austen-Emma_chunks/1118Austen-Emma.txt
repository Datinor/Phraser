This is very true, at least as far as relates to me, which was all that was meant--and it is very shameful.--Of the same age--and always knowing her--I ought to have been more her friend.--She will never like me now. I have neglected her too long. But I will shew her greater attention than I have done.   Every invitation was successful. They were all disengaged and all happy.--The preparatory interest of this dinner, however, was not yet over.
