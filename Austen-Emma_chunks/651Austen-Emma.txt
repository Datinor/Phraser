Yes, he has been gone just four weeks, as you observe, Miss Woodhouse, four weeks yesterday.--A Miss Hawkins!--Well, I had always rather fancied it would be some young lady hereabouts. not that I ever--Cole once whispered to me--but I immediately said, 'No, Mr. Elton is a most worthy young man--but'--In short, I do not think I am particularly quick at those sort of discoveries. I do not pretend to it. What is before me, I see. At the same time, nobody could wonder if Elton should have aspired--Miss Woodhouse lets me chatter on, so goodhumouredly.
