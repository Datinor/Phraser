As a counsellor she was not wanted. but as an approver, (a much safer character,) she was truly welcome. Her approbation, at once general and minute, warm and incessant, could not but please. and for another halfhour they were all walking to and fro, between the different rooms, some suggesting, some attending, and all in happy enjoyment of the future.
