Perry was very anxious that he should have a carriage, and came to my mother in great spirits one morning because she thought she had prevailed. Jane, don't you remember grandmama's telling us of it when we got home? I forget where we had been walking to--very likely to Randalls. yes, I think it was to Randalls. Perry was always particularly fond of my mother--indeed I do not know who is not--and she had mentioned it to her in confidence.
