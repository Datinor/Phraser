  Emma scarcely heard what was said.--Her mind was divided between two ideas--her own former conversations with him about Miss Fairfax. and poor Harriet.--and for some time she could only exclaim, and require confirmation, repeated confirmation.   Well, this is a circumstance which I must think of at least half a day, before I can at all comprehend it. What!--engaged to her all the winter--before either of them came to Highbury? Engaged since October,--secretly engaged.--It has hurt me, Emma, very much.
